Our Story

How we Met:

Two souls united in unlikely circumstances, but ones neither of us would change. Obi-Wan: I didn't expect to fall in love with someone so quickly, nor did I expect to be loved so thoroughly in return. We met through Mutual (she made the first move!) and when I saw her I was immediately enamored by her smile. So I swiped. Our first real conversation was a study of the scriptures, and I felt such peace and happiness that I knew that she was someone special. As time passed, though admittedly less time than a lot of people expected, I realized a few things. That love was beautiful. That the Spirit was strong when we were together. That I knew her in the Pre-Mortal Life and had promised to find her. And that I would marry her. I don't regret a single thing. I want to love and cherish her for time and all eternity. She is someone kind, brave, sweet, beautiful, spiritual, amazing and so much more. She is My Eternity.

The Proposal:

A surprise to be sure but a welcome one! With the help of some friends and her sister, we planned a photoshoot after church. From the temple to Laie point where we had our first in-person date. I faced her away from me and kneeled during the sunset, on the same spot that we started our future together. Much like the eternity we want with each other, her eternity band is a symbol of the promise we will make in the House of the Lord.
